About Barter System

The barter system is a method of exchange where goods or services are directly exchanged for other goods or services without using a medium of exchange, such as money. It is an ancient practice that has been used for centuries and is still used in some societies today.

In a barter system, individuals negotiate the value of their goods or services and agree on a trade. This system relies on a double coincidence of wants, where each party must have something that the other party desires.
